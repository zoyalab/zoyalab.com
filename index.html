Who we are?

We are a small team of experienced developers focused on back-end web application development.
The zoya.lab company has been founded in 2012, however members of zoya.lab team have strong web development background reaching early-2000s.

Whether you'd like to materialize your idea for The Next Major Thing On The Internet, implement a complex B2B application, create and execute a successful e-commerce website, or perhaps you're looking to support and extend an existing web application - we are here to help you.

What we do?

Back-end development

As a web software house our primary focus is back-end development - the heart and engine of every web application that covers core business logic that matters most from client's point of view. Technologies covered: PHP/MySQL (LAMP stack), Python+Django, AngularJS etc.

E-commerce

The e-commerce business is complex and fascinating at the same time. We've been working with a number of exciting e-comm projects which included product databases with 10+ million products. We can help you build either a small shop selling limited range of t-shirts as well as bespoke B2B e-commerce application.

Mobile apps

Mobile seems like a buzzword - everyone and everything goes mobile nowadays. In fact, it turns out mobile and web have a lot in common. Leveraging the best of both worlds we can build hybrid mobile app using web technologies that work exactly as a native app for Android or iOS.

Consultancy & support

Our experience has been valued by a number of clients who asked us to support and extend existing web applications, as well as provide consultancy services.
